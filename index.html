Area of Research: Estimation and Stability of Nonlinear Dynamical Systems with Application to Cyber-Physical Security

Anatolii Khalin is a Postdoctoral Researcher at the CentraleSupélec Rennes and IRISA Rennes, where he works with Cyber-Physical attack detection with Application to Smart Grids. He holds a PhD (2022) in Applied Mathematics from the University of Lille, a M.Sc. (2019) in Applied Mathematics and a B.Sc. (2017) in Mechanics from the V.N. Karazin University, Kharkiv, Ukraine.

His primary research interests include Estimation, Control and Attack detection, particularly for Cyber-Physical Systems, and IoT applications. Previously worked with HCI applications, CFD, and Modelling of AUVs (Autonomous Underwater Vehicles). CV (PDF)»
