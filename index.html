Area of Research: Estimation and Stability of Nonlinear Dynamical Systems

Anatolii Khalin is a PhD Student at the University of Lille, where he studies estimation and modeling of nonlinear dynamical systems with applications in the Human-Computer Interaction. He holds a M.Sc. (2019) in Applied Mathematics and a B.Sc. (2017) in Mechanics from the V.N. Karazin University, Kharkiv, Ukraine.

His primary research interests include Estimation, Control and Modelling, particularly of certain classes of nonlinear dynamical systems, Human-Computer Interaction applications. Previously worked on CFD and Modelling of AUVs (Autonomous Underwater Vehicles). CV (PDF)»
